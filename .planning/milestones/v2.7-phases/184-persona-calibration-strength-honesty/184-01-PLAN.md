<objective>
Extend the Phase-180 calibration harness with a style axis and add a persona-cell schedule keyed by PersonaId so all 24 personas can be measured independently — including the personas that collide on identical (botElo, blend) after retargeting.

Purpose: This is the measurement machinery for CAL-04. Without the style seam the harness measures vanilla presets, not styled personas; without PersonaId keying, colliding personas silently overwrite each other and the style-induced strength delta (the entire point of CAL-04) is lost.
Output: A style-threaded harness plus a new persona-cell scheduler and its fixture check, ready for the overnight sweep.
</objective>
