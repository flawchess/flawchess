<objective>
Extend `botDrawGate.ts` (STYLE-02) with style-specific draw contempt and a bot resign predicate, policy-only per D-07: a signed `contempt` knob shifts the accept target (D-09), and a new pure `wouldBotResign` predicate lets Light/Deep personas resign past a hysteresis floor while Human rungs never resign (D-08). Output: `contempt` param on `wouldBotAcceptDraw`, new `wouldBotResign` + named constants, updated module header, extended `botDrawGate.test.ts`.
