<objective>
Build the second background drain coroutine `run_full_eval_drain` that analyzes every
non-terminal ply of queued games at the 1M-node budget and wire it into the app lifespan.
It owns the new `full_evals_completed_at` marker, the ply<=20 dedup, the
preserve-vs-overwrite write logic, the LIFO interim pick with a guest filter, and the
yield gate that keeps the hot entry-ply lane fast.

Purpose: This is the functional core of Phase 116 — EVAL-01 (all-ply collection), EVAL-03
(dedup reuse), EVAL-05 (marker write), and the QUEUE-07 coexistence behaviors (yield gate +
gather-outside-session discipline). The existing `run_eval_drain` stays untouched (D-116-08).

Output: `run_full_eval_drain` + helpers in eval_drain.py, lifespan wiring in main.py, and a
full integration test file.
</objective>
