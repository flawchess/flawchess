Build the backend half of the per-TC card payload:

1. Replace `ClockPressureResponse` + `TimePressureChartResponse` fields on `EndgameOverviewResponse` with a single `time_pressure_cards: TimePressureCardsResponse` (Research Q3 verdict — fold into overview, no new route).
2. Implement `_compute_time_pressure_cards(clock_rows)` in `app/services/endgame_service.py` that produces one `TimePressureTcCard` per TC (gated by `MIN_GAMES_PER_TC_CARD`), each carrying 1 Clock Gap bullet (via `compute_paired_difference_test` on per-game `(user_clock - opp_clock) / base_clock`) and 5 Score-Delta bullets (one per 20% base-clock-remaining quintile, via `compute_score_delta_vs_reference` against a per-(TC, quintile) mirror-bucket cohort_score).

5. Add service-level tests in a new `tests/services/test_time_pressure_service.py`.

Purpose: this is the data spine. With this plan landed, the frontend can be wired in Plans 05-07 against a stable response shape.
