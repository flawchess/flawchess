## Decisions Made

- `_performance_rating` stays in `endgame_service.py` (not a new `elo_math.py` module) — only 1 caller exists; premature extraction
- No output clamping: Laplace smoothing bounds PR delta at ±802 ELO at n=100; Phase 87.5 "positive int" contract is satisfied by construction for sensible `R_opp_avg` values
- `ScoreGapTimelinePoint` extended directly (not parallel `EndgameEloInputPoint`) — backward-compat `= 0.0` defaults absorb all existing callers; YAGNI
- `_ELO_LOGISTIC_SCALE = 400.0` placed directly above the helper as a theory-fixed constant (not in the broader constants section); anti-pattern in plan: "do NOT introduce K-style calibration constant"
- Symmetry test (`test_symmetric_scores_around_half`) strengthened with magnitude assertions per WARN-02 from PLAN-CHECK.md — additive symmetry alone could mask a degenerate bug
