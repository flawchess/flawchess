# Phase 85.1 — Decision Log

## D-04 (Phase 85.1 revisit): Endgame Score Differences font coloring policy

**Resolved:** 2026-05-14
**Decision:** keep-zone-only
**Rationale:** Phase 85 D-04 deliberately chose zone-only font coloring for the Endgame Score Gap and Achievable Score Gap rows on the basis that the score-row triple gate was the right place to communicate statistical confidence, and that the gap rows should communicate practical effect size against the neutral band. Phase 85.1 has now shipped 95% CI whiskers on both `ScoreGapRow` instances (Plan 03, SEC1-11), which carry the uncertainty signal visually on the same chart row. Adding a triple-gate font color on top of that creates redundant information (whiskers + color), introduces UX dissonance for tiny-but-real effects (e.g. a clearly negative gap with whiskers that exclude zero, but `p` just above 0.05, would render neutral and contradict the chart), and reopens a locked design decision without a new failure mode justifying the churn. RESEARCH §13 explicitly recommends keep-zone-only on the same grounds; REQUIREMENTS SEC1-11 makes the new sig signal informational by default via the `InfoPopover`.
**Status of CI whiskers:** Shipped in Plan 03 — informational on both rows regardless of the font-color decision.
**Code impact:** None. `gapZoneColor` in `EndgameOverallPerformanceSection.tsx` continues to take only `(value: number)` and both `gapColor` and `achievableGapColor` call sites are unchanged.
