<objective>
Build the committed, reusable headless Node calibration harness that measures — over ~3000 stratified Kaggle "brilliant" moves — the raw Maia probability of the played move at each of six ELO rungs {600, 1000, 1400, 1800, 2200, 2600} plus a single Stockfish C2 grade per position, emitting a TSV (raw probs + derived gem-at-0.1 booleans + clickable `?fen=` links) and a drop-off summary. This is the empirical basis for the deferred ELO-scaled iso-rarity gem ceiling (Phase 163 D-08).

Purpose: SEED-094 / D-01–D-05. The headline artifact is the gem-detection-rate-vs-ELO drop-off curve. Engines and gem logic are REUSED (D-03 zero reimplementation drift), never re-derived.
Output: `scripts/gem-elo-calibration.mjs` + `scripts/lib/frontend-alias-hook.mjs` + `scripts/lib/gem-parity.check.mjs`; TSV + summary in `reports/data/`.

This plan does NOT modify `gemMove.ts` or `GEM_MAIA_MAX_PROB` — recalibration is an explicit follow-up seed (deferred).
</objective>
