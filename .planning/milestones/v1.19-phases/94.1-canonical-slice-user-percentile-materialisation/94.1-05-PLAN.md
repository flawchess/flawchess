<objective>
Per-user compute orchestrator. Two entry points, `compute_stage_a(user_id)` for the eval-independent `score_gap` and `compute_stage_b(user_id)` for the 3 eval-dependent metrics. Each opens its own session, runs the canonical-slice CTE built from Plan 03's shared module, calls Plan 04's `upsert_percentile`, commits, captures errors to Sentry without propagating.

This is the single non-trivial behavioural step in the phase — everything in Wave 1 is plumbing, everything in Waves 3-4 is wiring. The pooled-value formula (avg across cells, unweighted) and the below-floor representation (percentile=NULL + value stored, or no row at all) are the two judgment calls; both are documented in CONTEXT.md's Claude's Discretion section.

Purpose: The function called by Stage A trigger (Plan 06), Stage B trigger (Plan 06), and the backfill script (Plan 08).
Output: 1 new module; Plan 02's `test_user_benchmark_percentiles_service.py` + `test_canonical_slice_per_user_vs_benchmark_parity.py` turning green.
</objective>
