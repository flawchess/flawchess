**Per-user flow (RESEARCH Pitfall 9 ordering preserved internally):**

1. Stage A — invoke `compute_stage_a` (opens its own session). Internally:
   1. `compute_anchors_for_user` — Lichess-precedence (D-12) + chesscom_raw_rating capture (D-07 bullet 4) → UPSERT into `user_rating_anchors`.
   2. Per-TC `score_gap` percentile via `_compute_metric_for_user_per_tc` × `interpolate_cohort_percentile(score_gap, value, anchor, tc)` → UPSERT into `user_benchmark_percentiles`.
2. Stage B — invoke `compute_stage_b` (opens its own session). Reads anchors via `fetch_anchors_for_user`; iterates `STAGE_B_METRIC_FAMILIES` (7-tuple) × user's above-floor TCs → UPSERT one row per (family, tc).
3. Post-compute classification — single-round-trip DB probes `_classify_anchor_rows` + `_classify_percentile_rows` read both tables for the user, bump the right summary cells.

**Narrowing semantics:**

- `--metric` narrows the Stage A vs Stage B dispatch (score_gap → Stage A; others → Stage B + standalone anchor compute via `compute_anchors_for_user` to satisfy Stage B's `fetch_anchors_for_user` dependency).
- `--tc` narrows the summary printer + classification probes; the underlying compute helpers iterate all TCs internally (refactoring them to accept a TC filter is out of scope for Plan 06).
- `--skip-anchors` short-circuits the standalone anchor compute when a Stage B family is narrowed; documented to be a no-op when score_gap is in scope.

**Three-bucket percentile classification** (replaces the prior two-state upserted/skipped):

- **included** — row present in `user_benchmark_percentiles` with non-NULL percentile (user passed the per-TC pool inclusion floor AND the cohort CDF has a cell at their rounded anchor).
- **floor_rej** — no row written. The per-TC pooled CTE emitted no row because the user is below the metric's per-TC inclusion floor (e.g. < 30 endgames in the recent-1000 × 36-month pool, or no above-floor anchor in that TC).
- **suppressed** — row written with `percentile=NULL`. User passed the per-TC floor but the cohort CDF has no cell at their rounded anchor (`interpolate_cohort_percentile` returned None). This is the **new** bucket Plan 04 added — suppression now happens at the CDF lookup, not at the inclusion floor.

The three buckets are mutually exclusive at the (user, metric, TC) granularity.
