<objective>
Create the `game_best_moves` sibling table (peer to `game_flaws`) and its Alembic migration: one candidate
row per out-of-book best-move ply, storing `maia_prob` (float) plus raw Stockfish best/second centipawns and
mate flags (ints), keyed uniquely on `(game_id, ply)` with a CASCADE FK to games. The table stores candidates
only — never a gem/great boolean (GEMS-01/D-4); classification is query-time (GEMS-07).

Purpose: This is the persistence layer the whole milestone hangs on — Plan 05 writes rows here during
eval-apply, and Phases 175/176 read/backfill them. Storing raw cp (D-05) keeps the Gem/Great margins fully
retunable with zero re-analysis and matches the codebase's `game_positions.eval_cp` int-cp convention.

Output: `app/models/game_best_move.py`, its registration in `app/models/__init__.py`, an autogenerated
Alembic migration, and a DB round-trip constraint test.
</objective>
