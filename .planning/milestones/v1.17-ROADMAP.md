What began as a card-layout refactor became a statistical-rigor milestone: eval-based ΔES Score Gap bullets replacing the mathematically degenerate rate-based mirror-bucket peer-diff (Conv-Gap ≡ Recov-Gap by mirror symmetry); hypothesis tests + 95% CIs on the Endgame Score Differences card; the Endgame Skill concept dropped end-to-end and the timeline rebuilt as Endgame ELO from the Endgame Score Gap; a full Time Pressure rework with calibrated zones; and timeline inactivity-gap annotations across all six ordinal-axis charts.

## Phase Details

### Phase 84: Data plumbing — mirror-rate audit
Audited that mirror-bucket peer rates (`opponent_score` / `opponent_games`) are already exposed on `/api/endgames/overview` for Section 2 and Section 3. Per-type cohort p50 codegen dropped after the single-bullet-doctrine pivot. Collapsed to one plan (DATA-02).

### Phase 85 / 85.1: Section 1 — Games with vs without Endgame
Replaced `EndgamePerformanceSection` table and the Start-vs-End twin-tile with a 3-card composite ("Games ending in Middlegame" / "At Endgame Entry" / "Endgame results"), each with sig-gated tinting (Wilson on n≥10). 85.1 added a two-sample z p-value + 95% CI on Endgame Score Gap and a paired one-sample z (server-computed per-game `actual − expected`) on Achievable Score Gap, surfaced as bullet CI whiskers. Backend exposes `non_endgame_score_p_value` for symmetric gating.

### Phase 86: Section 2 — Endgame Metrics 4-card layout
Replaced `EndgameScoreGapSection` table + 4-gauge strip with 4 cards (Conversion / Parity / Recovery / Skill). Per-card title popovers split out from the section-level tooltip; mirror-bucket opponent-rate identity corrected in the Conv/Recov diff helpers; `_compute_score_gap_material` split into staged helpers.

### Phase 87 / 87.1: Section 3 — Per-type Endgame Type Breakdown
Replaced `EndgameWDLChart` + `EndgameConvRecovChart` with 5 per-type cards (rook / minor_piece / pawn / queen / mixed; pawnless hidden) in a responsive grid: Conv+Recov gauges, WDL bar, per-class chess-score bullet with Wilson whiskers + sig-gating, Games deep-link, `?type=<slug>` URL hydration. Design deviation: the two planned peer bullets per card collapsed to a single chess-score bullet (mirror identity made them render identical magnitude). 87.1 added a per-span ΔES Score Gap row (`ES_sigmoid(entry_eval) − exit_score`); LLM prompt `endgame_v28` → `endgame_v29`.

### Phase 87.2: Section 2 — eval-based ΔES Score Gap bullets
Retired the rate-based mirror-bucket peer-diff bullet (mathematically degenerate: Conv-Gap ≡ Recov-Gap by mirror symmetry; Parity-Gap a deterministic affine of `user_parity`). Each Section 2 card now carries a per-bucket eval-based ΔES Score Gap bullet anchored to the Stockfish baseline.

### Phase 87.3 (superseded): Endgame Skill v2 percentile composite
Designed Endgame Skill as a percentile of the Conv+Parity ΔES composite. Retracted on UAT review (no composite definition survived cohort-deconfounding / individual-interpretation / temporal-stability / median-coincide scrutiny). Replaced by Phase 87.4.

### Phase 87.4 → 87.5 → 87.6: Endgame Skill dropped, timeline rebuilt
- **87.4** — Endgame Skill concept removed end-to-end (card, payload findings, glossary). Timeline renamed Conversion ELO, fed by Conv ΔES through a frozen affine recenter into the Phase 57 formula. LLM `endgame_v31` → `endgame_v32`.
- **87.5** — Timeline rebuilt on the additive `endgame_elo = round(actual_elo + K · eg_score_gap)`, eliminating the sigmoid bias (benchmark Cohen's d 1.62 → 0.17). Renamed back to Endgame ELO Timeline, relocated under "Endgame Score Gap over Time". LLM `endgame_v32` → `endgame_v33`.
- **87.6** — Replaced the free constant K with a logistic stretch around Actual ELO so `endgame_elo + non_endgame_elo == 2·actual_elo` for every point (fixes the "Actual ELO between the two lines" invariant violated in ~88% of points by the trailing-PR estimator). Default to top-3 most-active combos by active weeks. LLM `endgame_v34` → `endgame_v35`.

### Phase 88 / 88.3 / 88.4: Time Pressure rework + viz refinements
- **88** — Time Pressure stats rework: per-TC cards with calibrated `PRESSURE_BIN_SCORE_NEUTRAL_ZONES` (4 TC × 5 quintiles) and asymmetric `clock_gap_pct` band from the Lichess benchmark cohort; restored per-card clock/flag-rate top zone (user-approved scope amendments); restored the "Average Clock Difference over Time" trend chart above the cards.
- **88.3** — Inactivity-gap annotations (Palmtree break glyph + compact label) on all 6 ordinal-axis timeline charts via shared `inactivityGapReferenceLines` helper; ELO Timeline defaults to single most-active series; "Endgame Overall Performance" restructured into one responsive 2-column card.
- **88.4** — Time Pressure responsive card grid (1/2/3/4-card); 3-stat "Remaining Time at Endgame Entry" header row; the four stacked per-bucket bullets replaced by one zone-banded zero-centered line chart with CI whiskers + hover tooltip.

## Milestone Summary

**Decimal / inserted phases:** 85.1, 87.1, 87.2, 87.3 (superseded), 87.4, 87.5, 87.6, 88, 88.3, 88.4 — the milestone evolved from a 5-phase card refactor into a 13-phase statistical-rigor pass as each redesign exposed the next layer of measurement problems.

**Key decisions:**
- Single-bullet doctrine (2026-05-12): cohort/p50 bullets dropped from Section 2/3 (rating-tier confound, redundant with ELO); one peer bullet per card.
- Rate-based peer-diff retired as mathematically degenerate; replaced by eval-based ΔES Score Gap anchored to the Stockfish baseline.
- Endgame Skill concept dropped entirely — no composite definition survived scrutiny; Endgame ELO Timeline stands in as the headline endgame-strength measure.
- Endgame ELO derived as a logistic stretch around Actual ELO (symmetric, invariant-preserving) rather than a free-constant or PR-based mapping.
- Time-pressure zones calibrated from the Lichess benchmark cohort, pooled across ELO within each (TC, quintile).

**Issues resolved:**
- Mirror-symmetry degeneracy in the Section 2 peer-diff bullet.
- Sigmoid bias inflating strong players' timeline ELO by hundreds of points.
- "Actual ELO between the two lines" invariant violated in ~88% of weekly points.
- Opening Insights query-plan regressions (JOIN-over-EXISTS + extended statistics, ~3,800× speedup on the pathological case).
- Production Postgres OOM during large/concurrent imports (hotfix via GitLab Flow `production` branch, PRs #99/#100/#101).

**Issues deferred:**
- Phase 89 (Polish — popovers, gating decisions, automation rules, 375px parity) dropped as not needed.
- In-browser visual UAT items for 88.3 / 88.4 (4 + 3 scenarios) — automated gates green, not blocking close.
- SEED-002 / SEED-006 (benchmark population baselines, zone recalibration) — dormant, gated on full benchmark ingest.
