<objective>
Build the **lease half** of the dedicated, token-keyed flaw-blob backfill contract (D-04): the
Pydantic schemas, the server-side lease builder that walks each flaw's stored PV, and the
`POST /eval/remote/flaw-blob-lease` endpoint that hands an upgraded worker one tier-4-selected
game's continuation FENs to evaluate at MultiPV=2.

This is a SEPARATE path from the live ply-keyed `_apply_submit` (D-04 isolation): the positions the
worker computes are continuation nodes along each flaw's stored PV — `(flaw_ply, line, node_k)` —
not game plies, so they have no `game_positions.ply` row and must carry honest keys. The worker
stays token-opaque (D-04a): it evaluates the leased FENs and will echo the token unchanged on submit
(Plan 04). Lines whose stored `game_positions.pv` is NULL or yields a <2-node walk are sentinel
lines (D-06) — excluded from the worker lease and written as `[]` on submit. Per D-09/D-09a lichess
%eval games carry engine PVs at flaw plies and are leased identically to engine games (no separate
PV-compute pass).

Purpose: Give the fleet honest, isolated work units without overloading the safety-critical live path.
Output: FlawBlob* schemas, a lease-builder helper, and the `/flaw-blob-lease` endpoint.
</objective>
