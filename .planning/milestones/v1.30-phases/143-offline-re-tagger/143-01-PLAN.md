<objective>
Parameterize the forcing-line gate by win-probability margin (D-03) and audit-and-fill the
GATE-03 (mate-priority) and GATE-04 (defender-branch-then-reconverge) unit coverage against the
exact ROADMAP SC2/SC3 wording (D-05).

Purpose: `--margin` is the entire point of RETAG-01 — every threshold change must be a
seconds-fast, engine-free re-derivation. The gate functions must accept the margin as an
argument (not a mutated module global), because spawn workers re-import the module and would
never observe a mutated constant. This plan is the foundation Plan 02 (live classify wiring) and
Plan 03 (re-tagger script) both depend on.

Output: a `margin` parameter on the two public gate functions, plus the residual unit tests that
close the SC3 multi-ply defender gap and prove the new margin parameter is honored.
</objective>
