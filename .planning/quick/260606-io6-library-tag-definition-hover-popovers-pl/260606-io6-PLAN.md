<objective>
Three interrelated Library-tab features:
1. Tag definition hover/tap popovers on flaw chips, with thresholds sourced from frontend constants.
2. A tri-state "Played as" filter (Either/White/Black) wired through Games list + Stats-tab statistics (excluding rating charts), front to back.
3. Rating charts in the Stats tab honour the time-control filter (render only enabled TC series).

Purpose: Make tags self-explanatory, let users slice Library by their own color, and make the rating charts respect the TC filter that already exists.
Output: New frontend constants + popover, a tri-state filter control, a `color` param on /stats/global + library endpoints already supporting it, and TC-aware rating chart rendering.
</objective>

**Tag definition copy (LOCKED draft — minimalism, no jargon, one sentence, threshold via constant):**
Define in `tagDefinitions.ts` as `Record<FlawTag, string>`. `{X}` markers below are interpolated from `flawThresholds.ts` constants (format helpers turn 0.05 -> "5%", 0.70 -> "70%", fractions -> "5%"/"1%", abs seconds -> "30s"/"5s"). Keep the rendered prose human (no "expected score"):

- low-clock: "Played when your clock was under {LOW_CLOCK_PCT} of your starting time (or under {LOW_CLOCK_ABS})."
- impatient: "Played in under {HASTY_PCT} of your starting time (or under {HASTY_ABS}) while you still had a comfortable clock."
- considered: "A flaw you spent real time on, so not caused by haste or time pressure."
- miss: "You had a strong move available and chose a worse one."
- lucky-escape: "Your opponent let you off the hook right after your slip."
- while-ahead: "A flaw made from a winning position, throwing away part of your lead."
- result-changing: "A flaw that flipped your game from winning (over {WIN_PCT}) or at least drawing (over {DRAW_PCT}) down to losing."
- opening: "A flaw in the opening phase of the game."
- middlegame: "A flaw in the middlegame phase."
- endgame: "A flaw in the endgame phase."

Constant -> display mapping (compute in tagDefinitions.ts, NOT inline literals):
LOW_CLOCK_PCT = TIME_PRESSURE_CLOCK_FRACTION (0.05) -> "5%";
LOW_CLOCK_ABS = TIME_PRESSURE_CLOCK_ABS_SECONDS (30) -> "30s";
HASTY_PCT = HASTY_MOVE_FRACTION (0.01) -> "1%";
HASTY_ABS = HASTY_MOVE_ABS_SECONDS (5) -> "5s";
WIN_PCT = RESULT_WIN_THRESHOLD (0.70) -> "70%";
DRAW_PCT = RESULT_DRAW_THRESHOLD (0.40) -> "40%".
(The severity drops 0.05/0.10/0.15 are NOT surfaced in tag copy — no tag definition references them.)
