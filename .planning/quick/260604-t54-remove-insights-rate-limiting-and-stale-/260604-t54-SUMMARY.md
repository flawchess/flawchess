# Quick Task 260604-t54 Summary

## One-liner

Removed per-user hourly insights rate-limiter and tier-2 stale-serve fallback; the tier-1 structural cache (keyed on user+prompt+model+opponent_strength) already caps fresh LLM calls to one per variant per era.
