<objective>
Rewrite `PercentileChipPopoverBody` bullet 2 to show concrete per-TC game
counts and metric values, per the locked CONTEXT.md decisions:

- 5 aggregated chips (score-gap, achievable, parity, conversion, recovery)
  emit a per-TC list with `<tc>: <value> over <n_games> games -> <percentile>
  percentile` lines (plus an "insufficient games" line for played-but-below-
  floor TCs).
- 3 per-TC chips (time-pressure-score-gap, clock-gap, net-flag-rate) emit a
  single-line `Based on <n_games> of your recent <tc> games over the last 36
  months, vs opponents within +/-100 Elo. Your value: <value>.` framing.

Purpose: the current bullet 2 ("Based on your most recent 3000 rated games
per time control over the last 36 months") misleads users into thinking they
have 3000 games per TC. The new framing exposes the real per-TC counts and
values that drive the percentile, strengthening the "recent-games basis"
disclosure called out in `feedback_percentile_chip_tooltip_disclosure.md`.

Output:
- `PerTcBreakdownOut` Pydantic model + 5 list fields on ΔES responses + 6
  scalar fields on `TimePressureTcCard`.
- Mirror TS interface + frontend `PercentileChip` rewrite.
- Updated Vitest assertions for bullet 2 across all 8 flavors.
- Bullets 1, 3, 4 untouched. Test 9 flame guard untouched.
</objective>
