Migration strategy (the primary risk — read carefully):

Chosen approach (two-phase, prod-safe, mirrors the autocommit_block prior art in
20260601_154355_84fd28051d7d):
  Step A (inside `with op.get_context().autocommit_block():` — CONCURRENTLY cannot run in a txn):
    Build a fresh UNIQUE index CONCURRENTLY over the full key:
    `op.create_index("uq_gp_user_game_ply", "game_positions", ["user_id", "game_id", "ply"],
      unique=True, postgresql_concurrently=True)`.

    keeps it non-blocking and table-rewrite-free.

    does take a brief ACCESS EXCLUSIVE lock, which is acceptable):
