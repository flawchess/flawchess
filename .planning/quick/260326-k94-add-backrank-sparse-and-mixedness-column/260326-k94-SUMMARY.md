## What Was Built

### position_classifier.py

Added three components:

1. **`_compute_backrank_sparse(board)`** — checks `board.occupied_co[WHITE] & BB_RANK_1` and `board.occupied_co[BLACK] & BB_RANK_8` counts. Returns `True` when either side has fewer than `BACKRANK_SPARSE_THRESHOLD = 4` pieces on their back rank.

2. **`_MIXEDNESS_REGIONS`** — precomputed list of 49 `(mask, y)` tuples, where mask is a 2x2 bitboard region (`_SMALL_SQUARE = 0x0303` shifted by file/rank) and y is 1-based rank index. Covers all overlapping 2x2 windows on the board.

3. **`_compute_mixedness(board)`** — iterates all 49 regions, counts white and black pieces via `bin(bb & mask).count('1')`, accumulates `_mixedness_score(y, white_count, black_count)` transcribed from Lichess `Divider.scala`.

`PositionClassification` now has 7 fields: `material_count`, `material_signature`, `material_imbalance`, `has_opposite_color_bishops`, `piece_count`, `backrank_sparse`, `mixedness`.
