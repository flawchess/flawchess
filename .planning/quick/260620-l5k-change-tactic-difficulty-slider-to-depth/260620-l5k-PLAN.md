# Quick Task 260620-l5k: Tactic Difficulty range slider (depth units)

## Goal

Convert the Tactic Difficulty filter from a single-handle "full moves" cap into a
two-handle **range slider in depth units** (0-based ply, domain 0‚Äì11).

- Unit: **depth** (= `game_flaws.*_tactic_depth`, 0-based ply). Confirmed in dev DB: min 0, max 11.
- Range slider with min + max handles (mirrors OpponentStrengthFilter).
- Presets: Beginner `0‚Äì1`, Intermediate `0‚Äì5`, Advanced `0‚Äì11`.
- `0‚Äì0` must be selectable (both handles at 0): `minStepsBetweenThumbs={0}`.
- Default preset stays **Intermediate** (`{0, 5}`).

## Decision (asked + locked)

**Mates obey the range** ‚Äî the Phase 129 D-04 mate exemption is removed. A mate's PV
depth must fall within `[min, max]` like every other tactic. (User picked "Bound mates too".)
