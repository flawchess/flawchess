<objective>
Make `games.full_evals_completed_at` the authoritative "analyzed" gate for the Library/Games
flaw-stats surface, replacing the per-call eval-coverage recompute in
`_analyzed_game_ids_subquery`.

Purpose: A prod DB-report investigation (2026-06-17) traced 135s avg / 49-min max
`fetch_stats_aggregates` latency under an eval-drain to `_analyzed_game_ids_subquery`

to RECOMPUTE the >=90% eval-coverage gate on every call. That coverage is already
materialized in `games.full_evals_completed_at`. Swapping to an indexed games lookup
eliminates the cold-page scan AND improves correctness (it rescues fully-analyzed 6-9 ply
short games the live recompute over-excludes).

Output: A cheap games-based subquery, unchanged call sites, reversal-documented comments,
dead-import cleanup, updated tests, and a CHANGELOG bullet. No schema change, no migration.
