## Problem

Section 2 attributes the term "Agentic Engineering" to Andrej Karpathy in February 2026, with a parenthetical "VERIFY before presenting — primary source needed". Presenting an unverified attribution to a named individual in front of a dev team is a credibility risk — if someone fact-checks live and it is wrong, the talk loses authority.

## Solution

- Search for a primary source (Karpathy tweet, talk, blog post) dated Feb 2026 that uses the exact term "agentic engineering". If found, cite it with URL and date.
- If no primary source is found, rewrite the line to: "The term has emerged across 2025-2026 in industry discourse; no single canonical coiner."
- Karpathy's "Vibe Coding" attribution (Feb 2025) is well-established and can stay.
- Do this before the slides are generated — do not ship an unverified claim.
