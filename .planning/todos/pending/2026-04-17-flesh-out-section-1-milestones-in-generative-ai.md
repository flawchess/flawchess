## Problem

Section 1 of the BA workshop doc (2017→2026 milestones) is currently a bare bullet list of years and events. 5 minutes is budgeted but there is no narrative throughline or "why this matters for the talk" framing. A slide generator will produce a text-heavy timeline slide with no connective tissue into Section 2 (Vibe vs Agentic).

## Solution

- Add a one-sentence narrative hook per milestone explaining why it matters for agentic engineering specifically (not just "this model shipped").
- End the section with a transition: "...which brings us to the two dominant modes of AI coding today."
- Consider collapsing sparse years into grouped eras (e.g. "2017-2020: foundations" / "2021-2023: assistants" / "2024-2026: agents").
- Target: one slide with 3 eras, not 10 year-bullets.
