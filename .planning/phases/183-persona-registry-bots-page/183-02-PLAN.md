<objective>
Build the missing bot OUTGOING draw-offer trigger predicate. RESEARCH Pitfall 1 verified that — contrary to 183-CONTEXT.md's framing — no `wouldBotOfferDraw` (or equivalent) exists anywhere; only `canOfferDraw`, `wouldBotAcceptDraw`, and `wouldBotResign` do. This plan adds the pure predicate + its tuning constants, mirroring `wouldBotResign`'s exact shape. Wiring into `useBotGame` is Plan 03; the banner UI is Plan 05.

Purpose: PERS-02 says the persona pins the complete opponent including its "resign/draw-offer policy". Phase 182 shipped resign but never the outgoing-offer trigger. This is genuine engine-adjacent logic, not UI polish.
Output: `wouldBotOfferDraw` + `BOT_DRAW_OFFER_SCORE_BAND` / `BOT_DRAW_OFFER_MIN_FULLMOVE` / `BOT_DRAW_OFFER_COOLDOWN_MOVES` constants in `botDrawGate.ts`, plus unit tests.

Resolution of open design question (RESEARCH A4 / Open Question 1): the offer predicate REUSES the existing `contempt` knob for the target shift plus three new module constants for band/floor/cooldown — it does NOT add a new `BotStyleParams` field. This keeps the 4 shipped Phase-182 bundles and their tests byte-unchanged (no re-tuning), and gives correct per-style behavior for free: Wall (negative contempt, welcomes draws) offers readily, Grinder (positive contempt, avoids draws) rarely offers.
</objective>
