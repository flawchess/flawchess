<objective>
Expose the Lichess-blitz-normalized ratings to the frontend by adding two nullable computed fields
to `GameFlawCard` and populating them on-read in `_build_card`, with an integration test.

Purpose: the analysis board already loads a `GameFlawCard`; adding the normalized values here (a pure
function of `(platform, time_control_bucket, rating)` already on the `games` row) means no migration
and no backfill. The raw rating fields stay exactly as they are â€” the header keeps showing the real
platform rating; only the Maia slider (Plan 03) reads the new fields.

Output: two nullable `int | None = None` fields on the Pydantic `GameFlawCard`, their computation in
`_build_card` (correspondence-checked first), and an integration test in the existing
`tests/services/test_library_service.py`.
</objective>
