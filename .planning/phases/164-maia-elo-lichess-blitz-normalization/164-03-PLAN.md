<objective>
Make the Maia ELO slider default read the Lichess-blitz-normalized rating (with a raw fallback) by
adding the two optional fields to the frontend type contract and extending `deriveRawDefault`.

Purpose: this is the payoff â€” the slider seats Maia at the Lichess-blitz-equivalent value (matching
Maia-3's training distribution) instead of the raw platform rating, while everything else about the
hook (ladder clamp, user-override lock, free-play path) and the raw-rating header display stays exactly
the same.

`MaiaEloGameData` (useMaiaEloDefault.ts); a read-with-fallback change in `deriveRawDefault`; new hook
test cases.
</objective>
