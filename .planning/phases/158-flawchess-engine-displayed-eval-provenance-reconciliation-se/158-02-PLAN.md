<objective>
Add one new pure lib module that is the single UCI-keyed eval-lookup for the whole `/analysis` page. It merges the authoritative free run's `pvLines` and the shared grading run's SAN-keyed `gradeMap` into one UCI-keyed `Map<string, MoveGrade>` with strict free-run-first precedence, and exposes `getByUci` / `getBySan` accessors so display sites resolve a move's eval by identity, not by which search happened to produce it.

Purpose: This module is the structural heart of SEED-087. By taking only `(pvLines, gradeMapBySan, baseFen)` — and NO MCTS pool grade — it makes "show a shallow pool grade" impossible by construction (CONTEXT.md LOCKED: the MCTS pool's `objectiveEvalCp` stops being a display source anywhere). It also resolves the one genuine wiring hazard: `pvLines`/`RankedLine` are UCI-keyed but `gradeMap` is SAN-keyed, so a naive compare silently drops every match (RESEARCH Pitfall 1).

Output: `engineEvalLookup.ts` + its test. Pure functions, no I/O, testable without jsdom or workers. Consumed by plan 158-03's `Analysis.tsx` wiring.
</objective>
