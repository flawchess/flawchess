## Decisions Made

**MOVE_TYPE_MOTIFS frozenset**: Placed directly below MATE_MOTIFS in source, mirroring its pattern exactly (D-02). Contains exactly the three move-type motifs; discovered-check and trapped-piece are NOT in it.

**Tier 5 architecture**: `TIER_MOVE_TYPE = 5` (named constant) ensures any real tactic in tiers 1-4 beats a move-type firing in `_sort_key`. Under-promotion at rank 0 in `_MOVE_TYPE_REGISTRY` enforces D-01 dominance without separate dispatch logic.

**D-08 approach (i) for under-promotion**: The 4 TRAIN and 4 TEST underPromotion rows all have Tier-1 mates (arabian-mate territory) that pre-empt the under-promotion tag. No % floor assigned. The fast-guard `_UNDER_PROMOTION_FIXTURES` (4 curated clean positions) serve as the structural no-regression assertion.

**trapped-piece → SUPPRESSED_MOTIFS**: The detector fires on 9 TRAIN positions classified by the D-06 v3 SEE gate as "all escapes lose material," but whose lichess label does not include trappedPiece (lichess uses a broader definition). Zero precision → suppressed, consistent with the existing capturing-defender suppression pattern.
