<objective>
Add the backward-fetch backfill path — the phase's genuinely new machinery. Generalize both platform clients to fetch older history (lichess `until`+`max`, chess.com newest→oldest monthly archives), orchestrate one Sync job as a forward pass followed by a budget-capped backward pass, persist a per-platform oldest-attempted cursor incrementally, and make delete-all reset that cursor.

Implements: IMPORT-03 (backward-fetch backfill, per-platform boundary, never-delete). Decisions: D-03 (Sync is the single trigger — nothing runs on settings save), D-04 (settings editable mid-import; running job keeps its start-time settings), D-05 (one job / two passes / one progress bar), D-06 (all backlog fetching newest-first), D-07 (stop only when ALL selected budgets full or history exhausted), D-14 (lichess perfType CSV including correspondence when classical selected — bandwidth optimization, correctness still guaranteed by the post-fetch filter from Plan 01).

Purpose: Raising the cap or enabling a TC actually backfills older history, capped per (platform,TC) budget, resumably.
Output: backward client functions, two-pass orchestration, cursor persistence, delete-all cursor reset.
</objective>
