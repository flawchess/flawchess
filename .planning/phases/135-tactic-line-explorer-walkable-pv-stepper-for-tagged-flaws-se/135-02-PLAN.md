  <behavior>
    - Given moves=['Nf5','Qd7','Nxe7'], rootFen (full FEN), tacticDepthRaw=1, orientation='missed': initial currentPly=0, position==rootFen, lastMove==null.
    - goForward advances currentPly to 1 and sets position to the FEN after Nf5, lastMove={from,to} of Nf5; canGoBack becomes true.
    - goForward at the last ply does not advance past moves.length; canGoForward becomes false at the end.
    - goBack from ply 1 returns to ply 0 (root); floors at 0 (goBack at 0 is a no-op).
    - goToMove(2) jumps directly to ply 2.
    - displayDepth = max(0, toDisplayDepthForOrientation(tacticDepthRaw, orientation) - currentPly); never negative even when currentPly exceeds rootDisplayDepth (short-PV / past-punchline case).
    - isPayoff is false at/before the tactic punchline (currentPly <= tacticDepthRaw) and true once currentPly > tacticDepthRaw.
    - reset() returns to ply 0 / rootFen.
  </behavior>
