<objective>
Build the Flaw-Stats panel (UI-SPEC Sketch 002-A): the panel shell `FlawStatsPanel` (title +
per-game/per-100 toggle + denominator pill + isError) composing three zones — `FlawStatsBand`
(Zone 1: four severity-rate cells), `FlawTrendChart` (Zone 2: blunders/game Recharts trend), and
`FlawTagDistribution` (Zone 3: tempo stacked bar with unmeasured remainder, phase histogram, and
the Opportunity/Impact sub-columns rendered from the real D-01 rate fields per D-03).

Purpose: This panel is half of LIBG-03. It depends on the D-01 backend slice (Plan 01) for the
miss/lucky-escape/while-ahead rates that Zone 3 renders directly — no placeholders, no client-side
derivation (D-02/D-03). The denominator pill makes the `% analyzed` + N explicit so the panel never
implies clean games where evals are merely absent (Success Criterion 4).
Output: Four new components. The panel receives `FlawStatsResponse` + `isError` as props; the
data-fetching `useLibraryFlawStats` wiring lives in GamesTab (Plan 07).
</objective>
