# Phase 80.1: Include transpositions in Move Explorer and Opening Insights stats — Verification Report

**Phase Goal:** Make WDL/score on the Openings → Moves rows and on Opening Insights findings reflect the resulting position including transpositions, not just games that played the exact candidate move from the exact entry. The row's `game_count` column stays as games-that-actually-played-this-move (move-played); `wins/draws/losses/win_pct/draw_pct/loss_pct/score/confidence/p_value` are recomputed over all games visiting `result_hash`. Same shift on `OpeningInsightFinding`: classification, Wilson confidence, ranking, displayed bar all switch to resulting-position WDL. The `n>=` surfacing gate stays on move-played count.
