       - `fastapi_users = FastAPIUsers[User, int](get_user_manager, [auth_backend])`.
       - `current_active_user = fastapi_users.current_user(active=True)`.
       - Optionally configure Google OAuth (gated behind `settings.GOOGLE_OAUTH_CLIENT_ID` being non-empty): `GoogleOAuth2` from `httpx_oauth.clients.google`.

Note: use `from fastapi_users import schemas as fapi_schemas` to avoid conflict with app.schemas.
