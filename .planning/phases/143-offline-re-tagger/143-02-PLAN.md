<objective>
Wire the forcing-line gate into the LIVE tactic classify path (D-02) by adding a thin
`_classify_tactic_gated` wrapper around the existing `_detect_tactic_for_flaw` kernel, and thread
the in-memory MultiPV-2 blobs from the eval drain into that wrapper at classify time.

Purpose: this is the crux of SC4 ("single classify path, no drift"). The live drain builds
`flaw_pv_blobs` in memory and classifies BEFORE writing the blobs to the DB, so the gate cannot
read blobs from the DB at classify time — they must be threaded from the call site. By routing the
live drain through `_classify_tactic_gated`, the re-tagger (Plan 03) calling the same wrapper is
guaranteed to produce identical results, eliminating drift. New games analyzed in the 143→145
window are gated at the provisional 0.35 margin (D-02a, accepted; self-heals at the Phase 145
corpus retag).

Output: `_classify_tactic_gated` + `_solver_color_for` in flaws_service.py, the threaded
`flaw_pv_blobs` param on `_classify_and_fill_oracle`, and a unit test pinning the gated-wrapper
contract.
</objective>
